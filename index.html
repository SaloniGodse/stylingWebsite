Important Facts About Camels!

  1. There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  2. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  3. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Important Facts About chameleons!

  1. Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads.
  2. Chameleons like it hot.
  3. Chameleons come in a lot of different shapes and sizes

Important Facts About Pigeons!

  1. Pigeons are highly sociable animals.
  2. Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Important Facts About Teddy Bears!

  1. September 9th is National Teddy Bear Day!
  2. Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  3. The world's largest stitched teddy bear is proudly American!

Important Facts About Pandas!

  1. THEY HAVE GREAT CAMOUFLAGE FOR THEIR ENVIRONMENT
  2. THEIR EYES ARE DIFFERENT TO NORMAL BEARS
  3. CUBS ARE WELL PROTECTED IN THEIR FIRST MONTH

Important Facts About Cicadas!

  1. Cicadas are not locusts
  2. Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans
  3. Cicadas inundate forests as a survival mechanism
